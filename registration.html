Registration

Important Notes:

  • Regular and student registration includes all technical sessions, proceedings, lunches, coffee breaks, and banquet.
  • For conference registration, please send an email to vinciinfo23@gmail.com with your full name, ACM membership, address, country, and email address. Please download and fill out the Registration Form and send to vinciinfo23@gmail.com.
  • If you are a member at HKUSTGZ, please download and complete the Bill Form, and send it together with the Registration Form. Please do not pay, just fill out the form.
  • Please write “VINCI2023 + Your name” when transferring the registration fee, and send the scanned version of transfer voucher to vinciinfo23@gmail.com.
  • The invitation letter is sent ONLY if registration payment is received.
  • For on-site registration, please pay your registration fee with UnionPay/Visa/MastarCard card (We do not support cash payments).
  • Notice that every accepted paper must have one regular registration.
  • For local Chinese registrations: 如需要开发票，请注册时提供单位名称、纳税人识别号及发票内容等信息.
