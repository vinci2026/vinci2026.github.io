Awards

To recognize outstanding contributions to the field and the community, VINCI ’26 proposes the establishment of an Awarding Committee to oversee the following categories:

Best Paper Awards: Recognizing top-quality submissions from each paper track.

Achievement Awards (New): Honoring long-term contributions to the field of visual information.

Young Researchers Awards (New): Recognizing rising stars and early-career excellence.

Service Awards (New): Acknowledging exceptional service to the VINCI community and organization.
